FAQ

About USDKG

What is USDKG?

USDKG is a stablecoin on the TRON blockchain backed 1:1 by a dual-reserve system of USD fiat and physical gold. Users can swap other tokens for USDKG, stake to earn 6.8% APY, or provide liquidity to earn swap fees — all fully on-chain on TRON.

How is USDKG backed?

USDKG maintains a dual-reserve: 55% USD fiat in FDIC-insured custodian accounts, 30% physical gold in LBMA-certified vaults, 10% US Treasury Bills, and 5% on-chain liquidity buffer. Monthly attestations are published via the USDKG Reserve Oracle.

How do I earn yield with USDKG?

Two ways: (1) Stake USDKG to earn 6.8% APY from protocol swap fee revenue and reserve yields — no lock-up, instant withdrawals. (2) Provide liquidity to USDKG pools (8.2%–14.6% APY) and earn a proportional share of swap fees.

What tokens can I swap for USDKG?

USDKG Swap supports USDT, TRX, WBTC, WETH, BTT, SUN, and all major TRON-native tokens. Swap fee is just 0.05% — one of the lowest in TRON DeFi. Additional token pairs are added via governance vote.

Is USDKG always $1.00?

Yes. USDKG maintains a hard $1.00 peg via its dual-reserve system and on-chain arbitrage mechanisms. The Reserve Oracle continuously verifies that all USDKG in circulation is fully backed. If the peg deviates, rebalancing mechanisms restore it automatically.

Why TRON for USDKG?

TRON hosts the world's largest USDT supply — over $78B — making it the most liquid stablecoin network in crypto. Near-zero transaction fees mean swapping and staking USDKG costs less than $0.01. USDKG is built where stablecoin activity already lives.
